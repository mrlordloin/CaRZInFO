List of some of the amazing car Companies

    • Devel Sixteen, known in full as Defining Extreme Vehicles Car Industry LLC, is a Dubai-based car manufacturer that has gained notoriety in 2013 for its Devel Sixteen hypercar concept, which is claimed to produce 5,000 hp (3.73 MW) from a 12.3 liter quad-turbocharged V16 engine, do 0-60 mph in 1.8 seconds, and reach a top speed of 348 mph (560 km/h). The engine is made by Steve Morris Engines.

    • The company was founded in 1994 in Sweden by Christian von Koenigsegg, with the intention of producing a "world-class" supercar. In March 2009 the Koenigsegg CCXR was chosen by Forbes to be one of the most beautiful cars in history. On June 12, 2009 the media reported that Koenigsegg Group, consisting of Koenigsegg Automotive AB, Christian von Koenigsegg, Bård Eker and a group of investors led by Mark Bishop had signed a letter of intent with Saab to take over the brand from General Motors. General Motors confirmed on June 16 that they had chosen Koenigsegg Group as the buyer of Saab Automobile.

  • Hennessey Performance Engineering is an American tuning house specializing in modifying sports and super cars from several brands like Ferrari, Porsche, McLaren, Chevrolet, Dodge, Cadillac, Lotus, Jeep, Ford, GMC, Lincoln and Lexus. Established in 1991 by John Hennessey, their main facility is located west of Houston, Texas. This firm focuses on mechanical component modification for creating high-powered cars. Besides performance automobiles, they also tune sport utility vehicles such as Ford Raptors and Jeep Cherokees. They also work on luxury cars like Bentleys and muscle cars like Dodge Charger and Challenger.

  • Automobiles Ettore Bugatti was a French car manufacturer of high-performance automobiles, founded in 1909 in the then German city of Molsheim, Alsace by Italian-born Ettore Bugatti. In the 1990s, an Italian entrepreneur revived it as a builder of limited production exclusive sports cars. Today, the name is owned by German automobile manufacturing group Volkswagen.

  • 9ff is a German car tuning company, based in Dortmund, founded by Jan Fatthauer in 2001, specializing in converting stock Porsches into street legal racing machines, either as fully tuned cars, or selling individual parts or kits. The 9ff GT9 was replaced by the GT9-R, offering up to 1,120 bhp (840 kW; 1,140 PS). It was designed to take the speed record for a street legal car, with a claimed speed of 420 km/h (260 mph).
